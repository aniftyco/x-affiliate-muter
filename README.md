# X Affiliate Muter

> Automatically mute every account listed under a company’s Affiliates tab on X (Twitter).

### Usage

1. Go to chrome://extensions.
1. Enable Developer mode.
1. Click “Load unpacked”, select your x-affiliate-muter folder.
1. Make sure you’re logged into X (web).
1. Visit a company profile and click the Affiliates tab.
1. The “Mute all affiliates” button appears automatically.
1. Click it, confirm, and it will walk through the list and mute everyone it can see.
1. No manual token copying, no extra setup—just install, go to Affiliates, click the button.
